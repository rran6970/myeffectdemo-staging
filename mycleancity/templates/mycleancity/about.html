Our Mission

A national multi-pillar program that collaborates with environmentally committed organizations to engage, enable and evaluate youth volunteer actions. Our goal is to build a sustainable future.

To provide measurable results with an environmental leadership program run by YOUth for YOUth.



Our Approach

Transforming community engagement into a results driven and rewarding experience.

  • Results Driven: My Clean City strives to quantify youth impact by recording their volunteer results and crediting youth and organizations that assist in achieving environmental goals.
  • Rewarding Experience: My Clean City caters to the 21st century volunteer; providing a platform that engages goal-oriented mindsets and adds personal motivation to volunteer experiences.

Together we are working to bridge the gap between youth, local community resources and corporate leaders to improve Canada's environment.

Our Model

My Clean City is a unique national leadership program that engages and rewards youth who create positive environmental impact.

We strive to shift the attitude of volunteer work to volunteer play!

Take Action, Earn Rewards

  1. Young leaders form Clean Teams with their peers and serve as Clean Ambassadors in their school and communities.
  2. Clean Teams align with local environmental organizations to present existing environmental programs as challenges to their peers through My Clean City's interface.
  3. Clean Teams mobilize their peers to participate in their environmental challenges with host organizations
  4. Individuals and Clean Champions go to host organizations to complete their challenges and earn CleanCreds based on volunteer hours and impact which are recorded to their profile.
  5. Each Individual and Clean Champions’ user profile records the volunteer’s progress, completed challenges via CleanCreds and quantifies that volunteer’s impact. CleanCreds CleanCreds will unlock rewards and special opportunities for My Clean City members.

Our Founder

My Clean City was founded by Kelly Lovell, a Canadian motivational figure, nonprofit & community engagement consultant and global youth leader. Recently recognized as one of the Top 50 Student Leaders of the World with an inauguration as a Hesselbien Fellow and being named Canada's 2013 Emerging Innovator by Ashoka and American Express, Lovell invests her time in empowering individuals and organizations to excel to their full potential.

Creator of The Kelly Effect, Lovell works to develop forward-thinking initiatives to address current issues, promote social engagement and build sustainable communities. Her track record of fruitful nonprofit projects is an example of her investment in others.

Lovell exudes a commitment to fostering leadership development and community change in all of her work. As a motivational youth figure she has graced respected stages including TEDx and WE Day, speaking live to audiences as large as 7000+ and spreading messages of empowerment across the globe.

"If I were able to define my life with one word, it would be passion; it's the driving force behind everything I do, but more importantly, it’s the essence of how I help individuals find success in life. I invite you to be a part of my quest for global change and inspiration." - Kelly Lovell, Motivational Public Figure and CEO of The Kelly Effect
