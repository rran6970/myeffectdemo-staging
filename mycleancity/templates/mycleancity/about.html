Our Mission

Provide a national multi-pillar program that collaborates with socially committed organizations to engage, enable and evaluate youth volunteer actions. Our goal is to build a sustainable future.

Create a leadership program run by YOUth for YOUth with measurable results.



Our Approach

Transform community engagement into a results driven and rewarding endeavour.

  • Results Driven: My Clean City strives to quantify youth impact by recording their volunteer results and crediting youth and organizations that assist in achieving community change.
  • Rewarding Experience: My Clean City caters to the 21st century volunteer; providing a platform that engages goal-oriented mindsets and adds personal motivation to volunteer experiences.

Together we are working to bridge the gap between youth, local community resources and corporate leaders to spark positive change.

Our Model

We strive to shift the attitude of volunteer to volunteer play! Catering to the 21st century volunteer, My Clean City transforms volunteer work into volunteer play making community engagement a fun and rewarding endeavour.

Our Founder

My Clean City was founded by Kelly Lovell, a Canadian motivational figure, nonprofit & community engagement consultant and global youth leader. Recently recognized as one of the Top 50 Student Leaders of the World with an inauguration as a Hesselbien Fellow and being named Canada's 2013 Emerging Innovator by Ashoka and American Express, Lovell invests her time in empowering individuals and organizations to excel to their full potential.

Creator of The Kelly Effect, Lovell works to develop forward-thinking initiatives to address current issues, promote social engagement and build sustainable communities. Her track record of fruitful nonprofit projects is an example of her investment in others.

Lovell exudes a commitment to fostering leadership development and community change in all of her work. As a motivational youth figure she has graced respected stages including TEDx and WE Day, speaking live to audiences as large as 7000+ and spreading messages of empowerment across the globe.
