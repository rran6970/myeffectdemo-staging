Rewards

What are CleanCreds?

CleanCreds is our rewards system. It is a currency that is earned by volunteerism and community impact!

How do I earn CleanCreds?

You will earn CleanCreds by completing #CleanActs with your peers in your community.

Here are some quick tips to boost your score:

  1. Impact: Each #CleanActs will have a CleanCred multiplier based on the impact of your actions. The greater the impact your #CleanActs has on our environment the more CleanCreds you earn per hour
  2. Lead: Sign Up as an Ambassador to lead a Clean Team. Earn 50 bonus CleanCreds for taking leadership in your community to mobilize your peers and make a positive difference
  3. Recruit: Earn 5 bonus CleanCreds for every Clean Champion that signs up to support your Clean Team
  4. Support: Help your friends by sponsoring their Clean Team. Sign up as a Clean Champion to earn 20 bonus CleanCreds
  5. Connect: Interact with us on social media to be entered into weekly contests and CleanCred giveaways. Follow @MyCleanCity1. Trend #MyCleanCity
  6. Register: Don’t miss our early bird registration. Sign up before February 24th to earn a bonus 50 CleanCreds!

How are CleanCreds Calculated?

Impact + Effect = CleanCreds!

CleanCreds are based on the volunteer hours donated to a Clean Challenge and the impact of your actions.

How do we measure your hours?

Through our super cool new app available at the end of March! Search for #CleanActs near you, register to track your hours and share your progress with your friends.

How do we calculate the impact of your efforts?

The impact of your actions is determined by our CleanCred L.A.W.S:

Land - the effect of a #CleanActs on our carbon footprint

Air – the ability of the #CleanActs to positively change air quality

Water – the impact of your #CleanActs on water use, contaminant levels and pollution

Species – the impact of your #CleanActs on the ecosystems and populations of animals

Result: a metric based score that measures volunteer impact
