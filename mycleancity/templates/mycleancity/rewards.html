Change Creds Calculation

What are Change Creds?

Change Creds is our rewards system. It is a currency that is earned by volunteerism and community impact!

How are Change Creds Calculated?

Impact + Effect = Change Creds!

Change Creds are based on the volunteer hours donated to a Clean Challenge and the impact of your actions.

How do we measure your hours?

Through our super cool new app available to download for your iPhone & Android! Search for Actions near you, register to track your hours and share your progress with your friends.

How do we calculate the impact of your efforts?

The impact of your actions is determined by our Change Creds L.A.W.S:

Land - the effect of a Actions on our carbon footprint

Air – the ability of the Actions to positively change air quality

Water – the impact of your Actions on water use, contaminant levels and pollution

Species – the impact of your Actions on the ecosystems and populations of animals

Result: a metric based score that measures volunteer impact

Change Creds Cheatsheet

How do I earn Change Creds?

You earn Change Creds in two ways:

  1. Completing Actions and making a difference
  2. Mobilizing your friends to get involved as a community leader

Here are some quick tips to boost your score:

  1. Impact: Each Actions will have a CleanCreds multiplier based on the impact of your actions. The greater the impact your Actions has on our environment the more ChangeCreds you earn per hour!
  2. Lead: Sign Up as an Ambassador to lead a Change Team. Earn 50 bonus Change Creds for taking leadership in your community to mobilize your peers and make a positive difference
  3. Recruit: Earn 5 bonus Change Creds for every Catalyst that signs up to support your Change Team
  4. Support: Help your friends by sponsoring their Change Team. Sign up as a Catalyst to earn 20 bonus Change Creds
  5. Connect: Interact with us on social media to be entered into weekly contests and Change Creds giveaways. Follow @MyCleanCity1. Trend #MyCleanCity
