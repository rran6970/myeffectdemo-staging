Students

Have the freedom to choose how, when, and where you make an impact.

Make community involvement about more than just volunteer hours.

Earn rewards for your efforts!

With My Clean City volunteerism can be fun and create new opportunities. We are a leadership initiative made for YOUth by YOUth.

Our goal is to provide YOUth with a portal to:

  1. Collaborate with peers
  2. Engage in #CleanActs and fun challenges
  3. Make an environmental impact

There are endless opportunities to express yourself, find your own volunteer opportunities, use the My Clean City App to paperless track your volunteer activities and participate contests with friends.

So what are you waiting for? Join My Clean City and transform your summer volunteer work into volunteer PLAY!

Why My Clean City?

Whether you need volunteer hours, you are a passionate leader looking for an opportunity to take action or you simply want to get involved in your community, My Clean City is has something to offer everyone. My Clean City believes in the potential of YOUth and created a portal to give YOUth the freedom to lead in community your way.

YOUth create our #CleanActs, YOUth choose the activities to volunteer in, YOUth decide which organizations to connect with and YOUth lead our program in your community.

With My Clean City you can show the world what YOUth are capable of and be creative doing it!

Engage – Connect with like-minded peers and community leaders who are passionate about making a difference.

Impact – Sign up and participate in #CleanActs to make a positive impact in your community.

Earn – Get rewarded for your effort by earning CleanCreds. The greater the impact, the more you receive!

Redeem – Cash in your CleanCreds for awesome rewards including gift cards, prize packs, and leadership opportunities

Get Started

Your Impact Your Way!

Participate as an individual or a team.

There are two ways to get involved:

  1. Clean Ambassador - You are a leader, form a new Clean Team with your friends or represent existing club, local team or organization as official My Clean City program leaders. Get your own team page to create #CleanActs, earn team rewards and receive exclusive leadership and mentorship opportunities.

  2. Clean Champion - You are a free agent, participate in the #CleanActs that interest you at your own pace. Earn your volunteer hours and redeem for individual rewards.

Become an Ambassador

We need ambitious and charismatic leaders to bring My Clean City to your community!

Our Ambassadors are our leaders. They inspire others to make an impact and take an active role in marketing and communications on behalf of My Clean City. Run by YOUth for YOUth, you are our face and voice in your city!

As a Clean Ambassador, you get to:

  • Choose how, when and where you lead others to make a difference
  • Network with local not-for-profit and grassroots organizations, and be a liaison to connect volunteers in your community
  • Lead a team and create your own #CleanActs
  • Manage your team page and have a hub to share your ideas and take action in your community
  • Participate in exclusive Clean Team challenges with our brand partners
  • Unlock special rewards and leadership opportunities
  • Become a top Clean Team and be invited to join My Clean City's executive leadership team

Get Rewarded

My Clean City wants to recognize you for your actions. Cash in your volunteer hours for awesome rewards including gift cards, prize packs, and leadership opportunities.

How do I earn rewards?

  • Take Action
  • Complete #CleanActs
  • Earn CleanCreds

How do CleanCreds work?

CleanCreds allow you to measure your impact and compete with your peers on a national level! It’s like volunteering where you get to keep score! Choose what you want to do, and show others your accomplishments! Become a TOP Clean Champion or Team and move up on our Leader Board.
