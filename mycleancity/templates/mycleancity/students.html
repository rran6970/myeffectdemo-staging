With My Effect volunteerism can be fun and create new opportunities. We are a leadership initiative made for YOUth by YOUth.

Our goal is to provide YOUth with a portal to:

  1. Collaborate with peers
  2. Engage in fun volunteer challenges called Actions
  3. Effortlessly track your volunteer hours and impact in your community

There are endless opportunities to express yourself, find your own volunteer opportunities, use the My Effect App to paperless track your volunteer activities and participate contests with friends.

So what are you waiting for? Join My Effect and transform your summer volunteer work into volunteer PLAY!

Why My Effect?

Whether you need volunteer hours, you are a passionate leader looking for an opportunity to take action or you simply want to get involved in your community, My Effect is has something to offer everyone. My Effect believes in the potential of YOUth and created a portal to give YOUth the freedom to lead in community your way.

YOUth create our Actions, YOUth choose the activities to volunteer in, YOUth decide which organizations to connect with and YOUth lead our program in your community.

With My Effect you can show the world what YOUth are capable of and be creative doing it!

Engage – Connect with like-minded peers and community leaders who are passionate about making a difference.

Impact – Sign up and participate in Actions to make a positive impact in your community.

Earn – Get rewarded for your effort by earning ChangeCreds. The greater the impact, the more you receive!

Redeem – Cash in your CleanCreds for awesome rewards including gift cards, prize packs, and leadership opportunities

Volunteer Tracking

Are you tired of carrying around your volunteer tracking papers? Misplacing your signatures and trying to keep all your activities straight to track your hours?

My Effect is leading a movement for YOUth to upgrade volunteering to the 21st century. With our mobile app you can effortlessly track all your activities and hours with a simple scan of a smartphone.

Best part is, our activities are 100% verified and don't rely on self-reporting.

What does this mean?

You participate our volunteer challenges called #CleanActs, track all your hours with our mobile app and simply press PRINT from your web browser.

PRESTO your summer work all laid out on a clean piece of paper ready to be submitted to your school on a silver platter.

Already have volunteer opportunities that aren't included as #CleanActs in our Challenge Centre? Email us the details and your volunteer coordinator with the subject line: CleanAct Referral.

Don’t forget to to register and download the My Effect App for Android or iPhone to start tracking those hours!

Become an Ambassador

We need ambitious and charismatic leaders to bring My Effect to your community!

Our Ambassadors are our leaders. They inspire others to make an impact and take an active role in marketing and communications on behalf of My Effect. Run by YOUth for YOUth, you are our face and voice in your city!

Change Creds allow you to measure your impact and compete with your peers on a national level! It’s like volunteering where you get to keep score! Choose what you want to do, and show others your accomplishments! Become a TOP Catalyst or Team and move up on our Leader Board.
