Why Join The Team?

YOU want to make a difference but YOU DON'T want to be told how and what to do! My Clean City was created for YOUth who want to show the world all they can do and be CREATIVE doing it!

We are a portal for you to connect with like-minded peers, leading corporations, not-for-profit environmental organizations, and scholarship organizations - AND all through one avenue!

My Clean City provides a social media powered program to showcase yourself and your achievements to a multitude of organizations. By partaking in our Clean Challenges, you will gain valuable experience, participate with friends, take on leadership roles and earn volunteer hours for it all!

The great part is that you choose what challenges you wish to take part in, and which ones you don't! If you join a Clean Team you can even create your own challenges!

My Clean City challenges are FUN! There are endless opportunities to EXPRESS yourself, including challenges to create your own videos, participate in flash mobs, engage in social media competition and more

CleanCreds allow you to measure your impact and compete with your peers on a national level! YOU choose how and what you want to do, and others can see your accomplishments! Become a TOP competitor and earn a spot on our LEADER BOARD.

Finally, an EXCITING and REWARDING way to make a difference!

Join the Challenge

Play as an individual or as part of a group!

There are three levels of play:

  1. Individual - You are a free agent, choose the challenges you want to complete, move up the leaderboard on your own and earn individual rewards

  2. Clean Champion - Sponsor a Clean Team or your school. Earn CleanCreds on your own while donating bonus points to your friends and staying in the loop with exclusive updates from their tea

    Earn a bonus 20 CleanCreds for supporting your friend

  3. Clean Team - Take a leadership role and become a Clean Ambassador with a group of 3-5 of your friends. You can even represent your school as official My Clean City program leaders. Get your own team page to create Clean Challenges, unlock special levels and exclusive opportunities.
