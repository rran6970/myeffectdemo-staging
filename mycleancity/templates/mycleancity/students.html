Students

What if you had the freedom to choose how, when, and where you make the difference?

What if community involvement was no longer just about volunteer hours?

What if...you could earn REWARDS and be RECOGNIZED for your impact!

With My Clean City volunteer work is a thing of the past. We live in a world where volunteerism creates fun and opportunity. We are a leadership initiative made for YOUth by YOUth.

Our goal is to provide YOUth with a platform to collaborate with peers, partake in fun challenges and make a difference all at the same time!

Are you ready to play?

Why Join The Team?

YOU want to make a difference but YOU DON’T want to be told what to do! My Clean City was created for YOUth who want to show the world what they can do and be CREATIVE doing it!

We are a portal for you. Connect with like-minded peers, leading corporations, not-for-profit environmental organizations, and scholarship organizations —all at My Clean City!

My Clean City provides a social media powered program to showcase yourself and your achievements to a multitude of organizations. By engaging in our Clean Challenges, you will gain valuable experience, participate with friends, take on leadership roles, unlock reward opportunities and earn volunteer hours for it all!

The best part - YOU choose what challenges you wish to take part in, and which ones you don’t! If you join a Clean Team you can even create your own challenges!

My Clean City challenges are FUN! There are endless opportunities to EXPRESS yourself, involving video creation, participation in flash mobs, social media competitions and more.

CleanCreds allow you to measure your impact and compete with your peers on a national level! It's like volunteering where YOU get to keep score! Choose what you want to do, and show others your accomplishments! Become a TOP competitor and move up on our LEADER BOARD.

Finally, an EXCITING and REWARDING way to make a difference!

How to Play

Every game has some rules, check out ours.

Play as an individual or as part of a group!

There are three levels of play:

  1. Individual - You are a free agent, choose the challenges you want to complete, move up the leaderboard on your own and earn individual rewards

  2. Clean Champion - Sponsor a Clean Team for your friends or school. Earn CleanCreds on your own while donating bonus points to your friends and staying in the loop with exclusive updates from their teams.

    Earn a bonus 20 CleanCreds for supporting your friend

  3. Clean Team - Take a leadership role and become a Clean Ambassador with a group of 3-5 of your friends. You can even represent your school as official My Clean City program leaders. Get your own team page to create Clean Challenges, unlock special levels and exclusive opportunities.

Become an Ambassador for Change

Create your own Clean Team and send requests to friends to join your Clean Challenge!

We need ambitious and charismatic youth to lead their peers in environmental change—make your city a #mycleancity

Our Ambassadors are our leaders. They inspire others to PLAY CLEAN and take on an active role in marketing and communications on behalf of My Clean City. Run by YOUth for YOUth, you are our face and voice in your city!

As a Clean Ambassador, you get to:

  • Choose how, when and where you lead others to make a differen
  • Approach environmental, not-for-profit and grass roots organizations you want to work with and create your own Clean Challenges
  • Get official My Clean City swag, your own team page and social media hubs to share your ideas and take action in your community
  • Participate in exclusive Clean Team challenges with our brand partners
  • Unlock special rewards and leadership opportunities
  • Become a #mycleanvoice and be featured in our national challenge
