Students

What if you could have the freedom to choose how, when, and where you make an impact?

What if community involvement could be about more than just volunteer hours?

What if you could earn rewards for your effort?

With My Clean City volunteerism can be fun and create new opportunities. We are a leadership initiative made for YOUth by YOUth.

Our goal is to provide YOUth with a portal to:

  1. Collaborate with peers
  2. Engage in #CleanActs and fun challenges
  3. Make an environmental impact

There are endless opportunities to express yourself, such as creating your own videos, taking part in flash mobs, and participating in social media competitions.

Why My Clean City?

YOU want to make a difference but YOU DON’T want to be told what to do! My Clean City was created for YOUth who want to show the world what they can do and be CREATIVE doing it!

Engage – Connect with like-minded peers and community leaders who are passionate about making a difference.

Impact – Sign up for #CleanActs to make a positive environmental impact in your community.

Earn – Get rewarded for your effort by earning CleanCreds. The greater the impact, the more you receive!

Redeem – Cash in your CleanCreds for awesome rewards including gift cards, event tickets, scholarships, and leadership opportunities.

Get Started

Your Impact Your Way!

You can an environmental difference in your community as part of a group or as an individual.

There are three levels to get involved:

  1. Clean Team - Take a leadership role and become a Clean Ambassador with a group of 3-5 of your friends. You can even represent your school as official My Clean City program leaders. Get your own team page to create #CleanActs, unlock special levels and exclusive opportunities.

  2. Clean Champion - Sponsor a Clean Team for your friends or school. Earn CleanCreds on your own while donating bonus points to your friends and staying in the loop with exclusive updates from their teams.

    Earn a bonus 20 CleanCreds for supporting your friend

  3. Individual - You are a free agent, choose the #CleanActs you want to complete, move up the leaderboard on your own and earn individual rewards

Register before February 24th and earn 50 bonus CleanCreds.

Become an Ambassador

Create your own Clean Team and send requests to friends to join your #CleanActs!

We need ambitious and charismatic youth to lead their peers in environmental change—make your city a #mycleancity

Our Ambassadors are our leaders. They inspire others to make an impact and take on an active role in marketing and communications on behalf of My Clean City. Run by YOUth for YOUth, you are our face and voice in your city!

As a Clean Ambassador, you get to:

  • Choose how, when and where you lead others to make a difference
  • Approach environmental, not-for-profit and grass roots organizations you want to work with and create your own #CleanActs
  • Get official My Clean City swag, your own team page and social media hubs to share your ideas and take action in your community
  • Participate in exclusive Clean Team challenges with our brand partners
  • Unlock special rewards and leadership opportunities
  • Become a #mycleanvoice and be featured in our national challenge

Get Rewarded

My Clean City wants to recognize you for your actions. Cash in your volunteer hours for awesome rewards including gift cards, event tickets, scholarships, and leadership opportunities.

How do I earn rewards?

  • Take Action
  • Complete #CleanActs
  • Earn CleanCreds

How do CleanCreds work?

CleanCreds allow you to measure your impact and compete with your peers on a national level! It’s like volunteering where you get to keep score! Choose what you want to do, and show others your accomplishments! Become a TOP competitor and move up on our Leader Board.
