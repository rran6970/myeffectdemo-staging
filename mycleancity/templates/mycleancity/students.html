WHAT ARE CLEANCREDS?

My Clean city is a team. Youth who join our team and take actions towards positive environmental changes in their lives are rewarded with impact and effort points.

Impact Points are awarded based on the environmental effect of the Clean actions, and Effort Points are awarded based on hours committed.

IMPACT + EFFORT = CleanCreds!

CLEANCREDS = REWARDS!

My Clean City challenges are just that—friendly competitions between youth for CleanCreds. CleanCreds translate into national and provincial rewards and recognitions. Emerging leaders and those with the highest CleanCreds will gain:

  • Monetary scholarships
  • Provincial recognition
  • National recognition
  • School-based recognition
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Leadership opportunities
