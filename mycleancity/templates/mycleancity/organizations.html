Why Partner With My Clean City

My Clean City is shifting the youth perspective:

Volunteer work → Volunteer play!

My Clean City’s fundamental goal is to strengthen existing environmental resources and organizations through creating an interactive portal, engaging youth and harnessing the power of a large national youth effort!

This cumulative effort will ensure for a cleaner tomorrow!

Our partners:

  • believe in the potential of youth
  • desire a more sustainable future
  • value bridging the gap between youth and community
  • wish to strengthen existing environmental efforts
  • foster leadership in young Canadians
  • empower leaders of today to find their ability to help community
  • move beyond awareness for environmental change and promote action

If you value these pillars we encourage you to contact us today to learn more on how we can work together.

What We Accomplish With Your Support

My Clean City strives to create a total attitude shift towards volunteerism. My Clean City is a team created by youth for youth, with the aim of redefining volunteerism as fun, engaging, self-expressive, and rewarding.

My Clean City is a portal to allow youth the freedom of choice—to choose how they get involved and at what level they get involved—and self-expression—to perform Clean Acts that are unique and express their inner creativity.

In giving youth the freedom to choose, and through utilizing CleanCreds—an incentive-based program—My Clean City will engage and harness those efforts for large-scale, national results!

A partnership and sponsorship will ensure today’s youth:

  • A platform—married with an interactive social media format—that provides young people a channel to find youth-centric, environmentally friendly volunteer and leadership opportunities
  • Motivation through earning CleanCreds that garner tangible rewards such as scholarship and mentorship opportunities
  • An avenue to gain volunteer experience, develop leadership as well as other valuable skills
  • The ability to experience intrinsic rewards of volunteerism
  • Presents a contemporary interface to engage those who are otherwise disengaged with volunteerism today
  • Networking and positive reinforcement opportunities for at-risk youth
  • An online profile, which records volunteer’s impact, allowing a reflection of one’s individual growth and fulfillment, and also of your workplace abilities for future job applications and interviews
